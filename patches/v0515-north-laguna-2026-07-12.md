## Performance

Full methodology and raw measurements are in
[`../benchmarks/north-laguna-v0515-r9700-2026-07-12.md`](../benchmarks/north-laguna-v0515-r9700-2026-07-12.md).

| Model | Final streaming TPOT |
|---|---|
| North Mini Code | **71.053 / 60.714 / 42.298 / 33.905 tok/s** at inputs 128 / 29,357 / 117,048 / 219,352 |
| Laguna XS.2 | **48.999 / 47.485 / 39.959 tok/s** at inputs 62 / 7,403 / 58,785; **29.270 tok/s** at input 220,277 |

North's fused-store OFF control was allocator/hardware-unstable, so no patch-082 percentage is assigned to
North. Laguna's reverse-confirmed OFF medians were 47.772 / 39.342 / 27.202 tok/s, giving **+2.57% /
+20.70% / +46.89%** at the three measured depths. Setting Laguna's full-attention pool ratio to 0.01
increased capacity from 875,509 to 1,009,385 tokens without a decode regression.

Kernel microbenchmarks measured:

- fused FP8 K/V store: **1.77× / 2.56× / 2.57× / 6.36×** at M=1/8/64/4096;
- fused sigmoid router: **3.4–3.9×** over the Torch fallback;
- Laguna BF16 gate GEMV: **3.2×** over the FP32 path;
- standard RMSNorm: **5–5.5×**, and fused query/key norm: **4–8×**.
