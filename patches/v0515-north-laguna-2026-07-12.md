## Performance disposition

  +20.70% / +46.89%**. The first ON pass was 49.875 / 48.597 / 41.597.

## Correctness and rejected paths

- Router microbenchmarks preserved expert IDs; RMSNorm was compared to the existing GPU fallback; the
  fused K/V stores were byte-identical at every tested size.
- North finished **34/36 comprehensive** (exact speed-of-light answer mis-scored by the evaluator plus
  a matrix-test miss), with coherent final context runs and tool-call PASS.
- Laguna finished **34/36 comprehensive** (the same exact-answer evaluator false-negative plus its
  pre-existing `-7 % 3` miss), capabilities **2/2**, tool-call PASS, and coherent deep generation.
- Custom/one-stage all-reduce and forced BF16 KV were neutral and not adopted.
- A fused Laguna softplus attention gate won in isolation but regressed end-to-end serving to
  37.085 / 36.232 / 31.423 tok/s, so it was reverted.
- BF16 collective is deliberately Laguna-scoped. Other HIP attention users retain the FP32 defensive
  default until their accuracy/performance trade is tested independently.
