# SGLang v0.5.15 base validation

## Target

- SGLang tag: `v0.5.15` (tag object `d80c135a`, source commit `f63458b5`)
- Serving tree: `/data/sgl-v0515`
- Conda environment: `sglang-triton36-v0515`

The base transition established that the existing RDNA4 series could be carried onto v0.5.15 without
losing its hardware-specific behavior. The current active membership is documented in
[`README.md`](README.md); the North/Laguna additions and later 072-to-083 disposition are recorded in
[`v0515-north-laguna-2026-07-12.md`](v0515-north-laguna-2026-07-12.md).

## Base replay evidence

The initial v0.5.15 base series contained 47 active patches:

- **47/47** patches applied strictly to pristine v0.5.15.
- The replayed delta matched the intended v0.5.15 tree byte-for-byte.
- Every patch was rejected on a second application except patch 026, whose non-unique Gemma video anchor
  is the documented exception.
- The patched import chain passed **29/29** smoke checks.

The integration preserved four load-bearing areas affected by upstream changes: HIP MoE routing, gfx1201
FP8 scale handling, Cohere2 hybrid-SWA classification, and the HIP Mamba radix-cache strategy.
