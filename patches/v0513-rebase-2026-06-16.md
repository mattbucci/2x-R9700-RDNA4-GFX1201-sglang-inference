   extended the try/except → `None` to the new `_moe` file. Swept all `aiter` imports tree-wide — every
   other one is guarded (`if _use_aiter:` env-gated, `try/except`, `if _is_gfx95_supported`, or in-function).
2. **stale `fuseep` import** (commit `9be0324`) — my 004 `token_dispatcher/__init__.py` "theirs" merge kept
   the v0.5.12 `from ...token_dispatcher.fuseep import NpuFuseEPDispatcher`, but v0.5.13 **removed** the
   `fuseep` module entirely (`NpuFuseEPDispatcher` referenced nowhere). Dangling import crashed boot at
   scheduler→lora→FusedMoE→token_dispatcher. Dropped the line; our FlashInfer try/except guard (004's real
   intent) preserved; `__all__` already matched v0.5.13.
3. **gemma4 vision-tower PP-wrap corruption** (commit `4dbc1a3`) — THE flag-#1/#3 bug. My 024 "theirs" merge
   dropped v0.5.13's `if self.pp_group.is_first_rank:` guard around tower construction, collapsing the

   `self.embed_vision`. Restored v0.5.13's PP structure with patch 024's `quant_config=None` on all 4 towers.
   Rest of `gemma4_mm.py` is byte-identical to v0.5.13 (native per-expert AWQ mapping used **verbatim**).
