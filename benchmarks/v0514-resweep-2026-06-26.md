## What the resweep caught (the reason to do it)
The initial promotion gate validated only coder-30b + gemma4 — neither is DeltaNet/mamba — so it **missed a
v0.5.14 regression that crashed the entire DeltaNet/mamba class at server-args init**: v0.5.14's new
mamba-radix-cache `extra_buffer` strategy asserts `is_cuda() or is_musa() or is_npu()` (FLA kernel), but the
`auto` selector picked it on HIP → `AssertionError: extra_buffer needs CUDA/MUSA/NPU`. **Patch 073** gates the
auto-selection on the same platform list so HIP falls back to `no_buffer`. Re-run post-073: all 4 DeltaNet
models boot + serve (`mamba_radix_cache_strategy='no_buffer'`).
